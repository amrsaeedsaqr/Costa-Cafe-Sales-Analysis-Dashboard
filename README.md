Project Title: Costa Cafe Sales Performance Analysis
1. Executive Summary
This project involved analyzing a transactional dataset for "Costa Cafe" to identify sales trends, customer preferences, and operational efficiency. I transformed raw CSV data into an interactive dashboard to provide actionable insights for inventory management and shift scheduling.

2. Technical Process (The "How")
Data Cleaning: Handled missing values in the card column (identifying cash vs. card transactions) and standardized coffee_name entries.

Feature Engineering: Derived new columns from the datetime field, including Day Name, Month, and Shift (Morning, Afternoon, Evening) to enable granular time-series analysis.

Data Visualization: Built an interactive dashboard using Excel featuring Slicers for temporal filtering.

3. Key Insights (The "Analysis")
Based on the dashboard, here are the professional observations:

Product Performance: Americano with Milk and Latte are the primary revenue drivers, accounting for nearly 45% of total orders (601 out of 1313). High-margin items like Cocoa and Espresso have the lowest volume, suggesting a need for promotional bundling.

Temporal Trends: Sales peaked in May (267 orders) followed by a slight decline through August. This indicates a seasonal trend where cold-coffee demand might be fluctuating or external market factors affected mid-summer sales.

Operational Peak Times: The Morning shift (510 orders) is the busiest, significantly outperforming the Evening shift. This suggests that staffing levels should be highest before noon to maintain service speed.

Payment Behavior: A massive 93% of transactions are Card-based, while only 7% are Cash. This high digital adoption suggests the cafe could benefit from a digital loyalty app or integrated POS rewards.

Daily Consistency: Sales are relatively stable across the week, with Tuesday (206) being the strongest day and Sunday (172) the slowest.

4. Business Recommendations
Inventory Optimization: Increase milk and espresso bean stock levels for the Morning shift to accommodate the high demand for Lattes and Americanos.

Marketing: Launch a "Slow Sunday" discount or a "Cocoa/Hot Chocolate" promotion during evening shifts to boost sales in underperforming categories and time slots.

Financial Strategy: Since cash usage is minimal, the business could explore "Cashless" hours to simplify end-of-day reconciliation.
